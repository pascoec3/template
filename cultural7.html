Conclusion

In conclusion, a major social issue today relating to Call of Duty is the present conditions of players and how they act in real life. The development of these different entertaining activities has lead to the depictions of Terrorism, destruction, violence, stratagem, teamwork, hand-eyed coordination, and bloodshed. There are multiple disadvantages but also advantages of playing shooter games. There are many other issues that relate to this artifact of popular culture. Call of Duty has become very important in today’s society. Many proposals to end production are a significant type of research and documents in the technical workplace. There are many discussions today about how children, teens, young adults, and adults can perform in high-pressure situations with control. It is quite possible to still remain in control even in the face of adversity and tense situations. People can still keep their composure without getting angry enraged resulting in possible dangerous situations. Those who can succeed without become irritated can still act a different way in real life, with a slight chip on their shoulder and have an edge of rigidness. It is enjoyable to be in the life of a first person shooter, to experience first hand what it is like to be shooting A.I. weapons and to ‘kill’ someone. People who realize it’s just a game and not real life are well in control of their life and should have no issues to control their issues outside of the gaming world. There are possible solutions to these issues; the best option would be to have bills written to oppose the uprisings and possible violence that follows of video games. This particular video game depicts mostly teenagers and young adults. These groups of people already have much stress on their lives growing up with several changes happening in their lives and the responsibility of growing up in the world. Call of Duty is still an amazingly written series of video games. I myself own every version of the series and enjoy every new update that comes out. When a new version arrives in stores, 1000s of players will wait in line to be the first ones to play it and to post reviews and new ideas. Concluding, There is still a danger that those who play may not fully understand the consequences of their actions they display in real life that reflect the actions played in video games.
